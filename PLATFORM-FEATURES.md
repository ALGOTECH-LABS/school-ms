## 🧭 Role‑based portals (one system, six tailored experiences)

Every user signs in to a workspace built for their job — no clutter, no wrong‑door access.

| Portal | Who | What they do |
|---|---|---|
| **Administrator** | School management | Runs the whole institution — users, academics, exams, finance, settings |
| **Teacher/Lecturer** | Academic staff | Attendance, marks, syllabus, assignments, quizzes, courses |
| **Accountant** | Finance office | Full finance & accounting suite, payroll |
| **Librarian** | Library | Book catalogue and issue tracking |
| **Parent** | Guardians | Track their child's attendance, results, fees, notices |
| **Student** | Learners | Courses, assignments, exams, results, fees, timetable |

**Why it matters:** the right person sees the right tools — secure, role‑aware access out of the box.

## 🎓 1. E‑Learning & Digital Classroom

A complete online learning environment — courses, coursework, and online exams, all connected.

- **Online Courses (LMS):** structured **Course → Topics → Lessons → Materials**, a rich lesson editor, downloadable resources (PDF, slides, docs, images, video links), and a single **Course Hub** per class tying together syllabus, lessons, and assignments.
- **Assignments & Coursework:** full lifecycle — **set → submit → grade → hand back** — with deadlines, file uploads, written feedback, and a live submission tracker (*"12/24 submitted · 4 graded"*).
- **Online Exams, CATs & Question Bank:** a reusable question bank feeds an auto‑generating quiz engine. Types: **Multiple Choice, True/False, Short Answer, Essay**. **Automatic grading** for objective questions; essays route to the teacher. Optional **exam timer + deadline** (secure server‑side countdown that auto‑submits and hard‑closes).

**Why it matters:** run classes and exams online at scale, with instant results and real academic integrity.

---

## 📝 2. Examination & Assessment

Everything needed to plan, run, and publish assessment — online *and* on paper.

- **Exam categories** (CATs, mid‑term, finals) and **offline/paper exam** records.
- **Marks entry** per class/section/subject/exam, with a configurable **grading scale**.
- **Gradebooks** — consolidated per‑student results.
- **Admit cards** — generate and print for exams.
- **Academic transcripts** — subjects × exam categories, per‑subject average, overall average, **GPA**, and final grade, exported as a **branded PDF** with signature lines. **Missing‑marks aware** (flags incomplete records as provisional).

**Why it matters:** from mark sheet to GPA transcript in a few clicks — no spreadsheets, no manual totals.

---

## 💰 3. Finance & Accounting

A comprehensive suite that runs the school's money end‑to‑end and generates real statements.

- **Fees & invoicing:** define **fee heads** → combine into **fee structures** per class → **auto‑generate invoices** for every enrolled student in one click.
- **Payments:** record full/partial payments with **discounts & fines**, printable **receipts**, per‑student **statements**, and built‑in **overpayment protection** (totals always reconcile: Billed − Collected = Outstanding).
- **Live finance dashboard:** KPI cards (Collected this month, Outstanding, Total income, Net), income/expense by category, and a recent‑transactions feed.
- **Statements & reports:** **Income/P&L**, **Fee Collection by class**, **Defaulters**, and a **Daybook** (ledger with running balance) — all date/class filterable and printable.
- **Expenses & other income:** categorised, with vendor details and **receipt attachments**.
- **Payroll:** staff **salary structures** (basic + dynamic allowances/deductions, live net), monthly **payslips**, and branded printable slips.
- **Budgets & school projects:** track **budget vs. actual** live, and manage capital projects (budget/funding/spend/cash balance).
- **Accounts, cash & bank:** multiple accounts (cash/bank/mobile money) with live balances and inter‑account **transfers**.

**Why it matters:** bill a whole class in seconds, see the school's real‑time financial position, and produce audit‑ready statements — payroll included.

## 🏫 4. Academic Management

The academic backbone that everything else builds on.

- **Classes, sections & subjects**, plus **classrooms** and **departments**.
- **Daily attendance** — quick take, monthly views, and **CSV export**; visible to teachers, admins, students, and parents.
- **Class routines / timetables** — clean, colour‑coded weekly grid (today highlighted) for students, teachers, and parents; teachers see their own schedule.
- **Syllabus** — topics and lesson notes per class, downloadable as a **branded PDF**.
- **Student promotion** — move students up to the next class/session at year end.
- **Student planning calendar** — a personal dashboard calendar with **Day / Week / Month** views that combine the class timetable *and* assignment/exam deadlines; click a day to see its planner.

**Why it matters:** the timetable, syllabus, and deadlines all live in one place — students always know what's next.
